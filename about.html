About

Robin is a character in Fire Emblem Awakening
and one of three main protagonists of the game.

At the beginning of the game, Robin is lying unconscious on a field,
when they are rescued by Chrom's Shepherds.

Despite losing their memory,
Robin has great knowledge of fighting battles
and serves as their tactician.

Leading Chrom and the Shepherds through a long campaign
to protect Ylisse from the Plegians,
Robin becomes Chrom's trusted adviser for war
and ultimately leads to Chrom's victory over Gangrel.
